The kernel of a linear map

*** Back
The set of vectors in the domain, $V$, which are mapped to the zero vector in the codomain, $W$.

*** Extra
$ker(L)=\{v\in V \;|\; L: V \to W; \; L(v)=\vec{0}\}$
