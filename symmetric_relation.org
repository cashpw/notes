[[id:bc7c3e81-4498-43dd-9f65-b2c0e0cbf521][Symmetric relation]]

*** Back
A binary relation, $R$, over a set, $X$ which is equal to its converse, $R^T$.

*** Extra
Formally: $(a \, R \, b \iff b \, R \, a) \; \forall \; a, b \in X$
