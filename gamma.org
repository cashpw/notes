Gamma (Î“) is an [[id:1263eb22-a819-43e6-9ab4-d45f790b095f][options]] risk metric that describes the rate of change in an option's [[id:ef21ad22-553d-432e-a4e2-bdc404009c87][delta]] per one-point move in the underlying asset's price.

[cite:@WhatGammaInvestingHowItUsed]
#+end_quote

#+begin_quote
Gamma measures the rate of change in the delta with respect to changes in the underlying price. Gamma is the second derivative of the value function with respect to the underlying price.

$\Gamma = \frac{\partial\Delta}{\partial S} = \frac{\partial^{2}V}{\partial S^{2}}$

[cite:@GreeksFinance2022]
#+end_quote
